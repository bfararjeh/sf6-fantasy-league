Welcome to the league view! Here, you can create and join leagues, configure your draft, start the draft, and begin lining up your rosters. This view also let's you check out your league's history as well as a league chat. This page is live! Everything from forfeit updates, to draft notifications, and chat messages will appear in realtime. If details fail to load however, the refresh button in the header will trigger a manual refresh of all information.
The owner of the league has control over certain settings. The owner is the one who assigns the draft order, sets the forfeit for the loser(s?), and begins the draft. The owner cannot kick people out of the league, and cannot leave a league while people are in it. The code in the upper right is your league ID, share it wisely as anyone will be able to join!
Once the draft has started, you can view the next player to pick on the right hand sidebar. When it's your turn to pick you'll be notified via a sound and the view changing. You'll have to name your team first, but after that you can draft players as you please. Your league will use a snake draft. Meaning, if the draft order is A-B-C, then the draft turns will go A-B-C-C-B-A. This balances the order a little, so starting last isn't entirely a bad thing.
Once the draft has been complete, this page will change to reflect your team. You'll be able to view stat breakdowns for all of your players, present and former, aas well as switch back to the league chat as you please.
Good luck, and happy drafting!
