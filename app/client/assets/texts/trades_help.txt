This is the trades page. If no trading window is active, you'll see a countdown to the next one, as well as a list of all the windows throughout the year. Be ready, because the second the countdown hits 0, the trading window is open, and players can trade with each other or the open pool at the leisure! During a trading window, each player gets 2 total trades. These trades can be manager to manager (within your league) or manager to market. 
Manager to manager trades are subject to both parties agreeing: you can send as many requests as you want, and accept/reject any incoming request you receive. Be warned, If you send too many requests and 2 get accepted, you'll be out of trades with no going back! So make sure you mean your requests. Manager to pool trades are a little different. They're first come, first served: any player not claimed by any team is free for signing at any point during the trade window. If you're racing for a specific player, let's hope your internet is fast!
Lastly, you can also take a look at the trade history. This shows all trades that have taken place in the league from every window.
Happy trading!
